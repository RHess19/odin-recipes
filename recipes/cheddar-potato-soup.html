Cheddar Potato Soup

Description

This is my favorite soup recipe of all time. It has very little prep time, takes only about an hour to cook, and tastes great!

Ingredients

  • 1 medium russet potato, chopped into bite sized pieces
  • 2/3 cup cheddar cheese, shredded
  • 1 1/2 cups vegetable or chicken broth
  • 2 carrot stalks, chopped
  • 1/2 cup half and half
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion powder

Steps

  1. Place vegetable broth in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Place potato, carrots, salt, and onion powder in the pot of broth (seasonings are up to your personal preference).
  3. Bring the soup to a low boil and let sit for 30 minutes, or until potatoes are soft.
  4. Add the half and half and pepper (again, up to you on the amount of seasoning).
  5. Boil for 15 minutes.
  6. Turn the heat off, then add the cheese and stir until melted into the soup and the soup is smooth.
