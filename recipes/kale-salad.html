Kale Salad

Home Cheddar Potato Soup French Croissants

Description

UGHH!

Ingredients

  • 2 bunches of kale
  • Italian bread, chopped or ripped into crouton-sized pieces
  • Dijon mustard
  • Soy sauce
  • Mayonnaise
  • 1 can chickpeas
  • Ranch seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic powder
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Drain chickpeas and drizzle with ranch seasoning, salt, pepper, garlic powder, and olive oil. Bake @ 375 F for 8 minutes.
  2. Mix crouton-sized Italian bread bites with olive oil, garlic powder, salt, and pepper. Once chickpeas are done, add to baking sheet and bake both for ~10 more minutes.
  3. Mix mayo, soy sauce, and dijon mustard for a dressing (quantities are apparently up to you!)
  4. Massage kale with olive oil drizzle and assemble ingredients.
