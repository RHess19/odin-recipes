Description

NUMMY

Ingredients

  • 85g lukewarm milk
  • 60g lukewarm water
  • 6g active dry yeast
  • 5g honey (or sugar)
  • 25g EU butter, melted + cooled
  • 20g white sugar
  • 250g flour
  • 5g salt

  • 140g EU butter
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 2 T milk

Steps

  1. Dissolve the honey in the milk then add yeast. Set aside for 10-20 minutes.
  2. Add the rest of the ingredients in order, then knead for 4-5 minutes until smooth. Let rise for ~1 hr.
  3. Form dough into a 7x10in rectangle on parchment paper. Freeze for ~2 hrs.
  4. Tourrage: Form butter into 5x6.5in rectangle on parchment paper. Refrigerate.
  5. Place tourrage on 1/2 of dough block, fold dough over and seal edges. Refrigerate for ~30 minutes.
  6. Roll out the long end on a floured surface to 16in. Fold one end 2" up and the other und down to meet this. Cut all 4 corners. Fold the dough in half and cut 2 corners. Refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  7. Roll out long end to 15". Fold 1/3 of hte dough toward the center, then the other third over that. Cut 4 corners and refrigerate for 30 minutes.
  8. Roll out long end of dough to 1 cm thickness. Refrigerate for 45 minutes.
  9. Roll out dough to 4mm thickness. Cut into triangles and roll up. Proof for 2 hrs.
  10. Mix egg yolk and milk + brush onto rolled croissants.
  11. Bake for 23 minutes @ 375 degrees F.
